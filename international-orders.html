What is the return policy for international orders?
If for any reason you are not satisfied with your purchase please contact us via email and we will provide you with further instructions on how to request a return. We are unable to provide free returns for international orders at this time. Any return shipping charges will be deducted from the refund. If you selected to pay duties and taxes at time of delivery (COD), you will not be credited for original duties and taxes paid. Please note, you have 14 days from delivery date to return your product. Any styles marked as FINAL SALE, Beauty and Intimate products are final sale and cannot be returned or exchanged.

Estimated Timeframe for Returns:
Typically international returns from Borderfree can take up to 5 to 7 business days from the date you return your package to be delivered to the Borderfree hub in the U.S. Once they receive your return, they will process your refund within 1 to 2 business days. For any returns from the United Kingdom, please allow 10 to 14 business days from the date you return your package to Borderfree for your refund to be processed. The shipping carrier that transports the UK returns have a longer shipping window than other countries.

How do I initiate a return for my order?
To request a return for your internationally shipped order, please contact us via email. We will submit your request to Borderfree and provide you with return instructions as well a label for the return of the order. Once the return item is shipped Borderfree will monitor the return and ensure to issue a credit for the items upon delivery. We strongly recommend utilizing the return label issued by Borderfree for the return to avoid delays in the processing of your return. Opening Ceremony and Borderfree are not responsible for lost packages shipped using your own return shipping methods.

My order was returned to sender. What are my next steps?
In the instance that the carrier was unable to locate your address or was unable to deliver after multiple attempts, your package will be returned to the Borderfree hub by your local carrier at their discretion. Please note some carriers may take up to 6 weeks to return the shipment back to the U.S. Once received our team will be notified and then your refund will be processed. Depending on your country, please allow 2 to 6 weeks for the undeliverable package to be returned to the U.S for a refund.

What is Borderfree?
Borderfree is a global ecommerce solution designed to provide an excellent shopping experience for our international customers by presenting product prices in the preferred currency, offering competitive international shipping costs, and delivering the lowest possible guaranteed order totals. Our partnership with Borderfree ensures that our international customer can always buy from us with confidence.

How will I know the total cost of my order?
Just add items to your basket and proceed to Checkout. BorderFree will calculate and display the order total in your preferred currency, including shipping cost from our warehouse in the US to your selected international destination, as well as any applicable duties and taxes imposed by customs and revenue authorities in the destination country. Certain countries will have the option to prepay duties and taxes at the time of checkout in which case a final order total will be provided before you submit your order. If the country is not available to prepay duties and taxes during checkout, your order will be shipped DDU (Delivery Duty Unpaid). You will be liable for all duties, customs, and sales taxes based on the country requirements where the order is being sent. Upon arrival, a separate payment will be required to release your order. Opening Ceremony is not responsible for payment of this duty. Please consult your local customs office for more information.

Are there any additional costs that will be due upon receipt of my order?
As long as you were presented with the option to prepay duties and taxes during the checkout process, the order total presented at checkout is the exact amount you’ll be billed by Borderfree, and there will be no additional costs due upon delivery. In the instance that the prepay duties and tax option is not available to the Ship-To address/country that you entered, you will be liable for all duties, customs and sales taxes based on the country requirements where the order is being sent. Upon arrival, a separate payment will be required to release your order. Opening Ceremony is not responsible for payment of this duty. Please contact your local customs office for more information.

How much duty and taxes are charged on international orders?
Duties (or Customs tariffs) are set by the destination country’s Customs and Revenues authorities. The total duties are calculated based on a combination of factors, including the country of origin in terms of where the goods were manufactured and the classification of that merchandise as defined by a harmonized system adopted by the countries that Borderfree supports. International taxes (such as Value Added Tax, or VAT) are set by the destination country. You will be able to view the guaranteed total amount of applicable duty and tax/VAT for your order within the Borderfree international checkout process if your Ship To country allows for the DDP (Delivery Duty Paid) option.

However, if the prepay duties and tax option is not available based on the Ship To address/country that you entered, you will be liable for all duties, customs and sales taxes based on the country requirements where the order is being sent. Upon arrival, a separate payment will be required to release your order. Opening Ceremony is not responsible for payment of this duty. Please contact your local customs office for more information.
